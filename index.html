Transformers age of extinction

"Transformers: Age of Extinction" (2014) The movie is fourth film in the Transformers series, directed by Michael Bay. It stars Mark Wahlberg as Cade Yeager, replacing Shia LaBeouf as the new lead. The story is set five years after the previous film, with the Autobots in hiding and introduces new Transformers like the Dinobots and the villain Lockdown. The movie was a box office success, earning over $1.1 billion, but received mixed to negative reviews, criticized for its length and complex plot, though praised for its visual effects.
